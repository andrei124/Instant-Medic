About us...

Hello and welcome to Instant-Medic, an internet-application built and designed for people of all ages who want to know more about their medical issues.

People usually find it difficult to figure out what is the source of their pain or inconvenience and many of them have no idea about the possible diseases that may lie beyond their symptoms.

Furthermore, very few of them know exactly what doctor should they go to and which would be the treatment they might follow in order to get well.

Our application is meant to help people make the first step in solving their problems.


Easy to use

Instant-Medic has a very easy-to-use interface so that anyone can easily complete the process and get a report (based on the user's symptoms) about their medical situation.

In Archive, you can find information about over 50 diseases, their causes, risk factors, common symptoms and possible ways of treatment.
